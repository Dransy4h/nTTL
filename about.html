About Us

Building Bonds That Last

For over 25 years, we've been at the forefront of industrial adhesive innovation in Indonesia.

Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leadership

Founded in 1999 in Jakarta, Nusantara Tunas Langgeng started as a small adhesive trading company with a vision to provide Indonesian industries with world-class bonding solutions.

Today, we have grown into one of Southeast Asia's leading manufacturers of industrial adhesives, serving over 500 clients across construction, manufacturing, and packaging industries.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility spans over 15,000 square meters, equipped with advanced production lines and quality control laboratories that ensure every product meets international standards.

1999 Established

Our Journey

Key Milestones

Company Founded
